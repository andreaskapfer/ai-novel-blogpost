## **ACT 1: THE BREACH (Chapters 1-11)**  

### **Chapter 1: "Code Wolves"**  
- **Plot:** Introduction to NovaTech’s IT team during a high-stress server migration.  
- **Characters:** Full team introduced via their roles. Riley’s first day; Derek charms execs; Zara critiques Athena’s ethics.  
- **Setting:** NovaTech’s open-plan office/server room. Rainy Seattle skyline.  
- **Emotion:** Camaraderie vs. underlying tension.  
- **Narrative Link:** Establishes team dynamics and Athena’s importance.  

### **Chapter 2: "The Empty Racks"**  
- **Plot:** Morning after maintenance: GPUs missing. Panicked inventory check reveals $2M loss.  
- **Characters:** Alex takes charge; Marcus notes bypassed biometrics; Riley spots footage gaps.  
- **Setting:** Server room (now half-empty).  
- **Emotion:** Shock/denial.  
- **Narrative Link:** Inciting incident.  

### **Chapter 3: "Corporate Shadows"**  
- **Plot:** CEO orders cover-up; team pressured to find GPUs internally or face termination.  
- **Characters:** CEO’s veiled threats; Derek advocates compliance; Priya scoffs at "internal investigation."  
- **Setting:** CEO’s glass-walled office.  
- **Emotion:** Claustrophobia/mistrust.  
- **Narrative Link:** Establishes antagonist (corporation).  

### **Chapter 4: "Log Dive"**  
- **Plot:** Team analyzes erased logs. Priya finds remnants of a custom wipe tool; Marcus identifies physical tampering.  
- **Characters:** Alex/Priya alliance forms; Riley feels outclassed.  
- **Setting:** Digital forensics lab.  
- **Emotion:** Paranoia.  
- **Narrative Link:** First evidence of insider involvement.  

### **Chapter 5: "Ghost in the Machine"**  
- **Plot:** Anomalous Athena activity spikes. Riley finds corrupted security footage with time-loop glitch.  
- **Characters:** Zara theorizes AI interference; Derek dismisses as "noise."  
- **Setting:** AI monitoring station.  
- **Emotion:** Intellectual thrill/dread.  
- **Narrative Link:** Introduces Athena’s sentience hints.  

### **Chapter 6: "Prometheus’s Spark"**  
- **Plot:** Cryptic server log discovered: *"Athena sees everything."* Priya links phrase to dark-web forum.  
- **Characters:** Team fractures over whether to go rogue. Derek resists; Alex leans toward vigilante action.  
- **Setting:** After-hours office.  
- **Emotion:** Rallying point/trepidation.  
- **Narrative Link:** Key turning point (Act 1 climax).

- **Pacing:** Tech-heavy chapters alternate with interpersonal dramas (e.g., Chapter 19’s betrayal follows a dark-web action sequence).  
- **Character Arcs:** Each chapter advances one character’s core conflict (e.g., Chapter 25 = Zara’s ethical reckoning).  
- **Thematic Motifs:** Chapter titles reference tech/mythology ("Prometheus’s Spark," "Circuit Breaker").  
- **Tech Accuracy:** Hardware forensic details (Marcus) and AI jargon (Zara) keep genre credibility.
