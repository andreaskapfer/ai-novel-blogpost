### L1 Novel Outline: *The Nerd Group and the Mystery of the Stolen GPUs*  
**Word Count:** ~150,000 words (3 Parts, 30 Chapters Total)  
**Core Themes:** Friendship Under Pressure • Technology’s Double-Edged Sword • Betrayal & Conspiracy  

---

#### **PART 1: THE VANISHING** *(Chapters 1-10)*  
**Theft & Initial Investigation**  
- *Setting:* Hyperion Solutions, a cutting-edge IT consultancy specializing in AI infrastructure.  
- **Inciting Incident:** Midnight alert shows all GPUs vanished from the server room hosting "Project ATHENA," a classified AI system nearing completion.  

**Character Introductions & Dynamics:**  
1. **Alex Chen** (Lead Network Architect): Obsessive perfectionist with imposter syndrome; spearheads the investigation.  
2. **Riya Kapoor** (AI Programmer): Radical transparency advocate; suspects corporate sabotage.  
3. **Marcus "Wheels" Thompson** (Cybersecurity/Hardware Specialist): Ex-hacker in a wheelchair; comic relief with a paranoid streak.  
4. **Elena Santos** (Data Engineer): Company loyalist torn between friends and management; secretly dating VP Nolan Gray.  
5. **Simon Li** (Intern/Quantum Computing Prodigy): Naïve newcomer hiding family ties to a rival firm.  

**Key Beats:**  
- Security footage wiped; physical locks untouched → Implies an inside job.  
- Team operates unofficially to avoid corporate scrutiny.  
- Discover GPU theft is *not random*: Serial numbers trace to a defunct mining operation linked to **Project ATHENA**’s beta code.  

**Turning Point:** Wheels traces a backdoor to *Black Ice Collective*, an underground tech syndicate.  

---

#### **PART 2: THE WEB WIDENS** *(Chapters 11-20)*  
**Unraveling the Conspiracy**  
- *Expanded Settings:* Dark-web forums, blockchain-based GPU black markets, high-security tech expos.  

**Escalating Stakes:**  
- Team identifies **"Ghost Protocol"—** rogue code in Project ATHENA designed to exploit GPUs for quantum brute-force attacks.  
- Corporate leadership (including Elena’s lover, VP Gray) stonewalls inquiries → Motivations unclear.  
- Simon’s father emerges as CEO of rival firm **Cerberus Tech** → Forces Simon to choose sides.  

**Character Arcs:**  
- **Alex’s Obsession** strains friendships (burns bridges with Riya over ethics).  
- **Elena’s Betrayal:** Leaks intel to Gray, believing she’s protecting the team → Accelerates Black Ice’s countermoves.  
- **Riya’s Idealism Crumbles:** Discovers Project ATHENA’s military applications.  

**Key Beats:**  
- Second heist: Backup GPUs stolen during a conference diversion → Team realizes Black Ice *wants them to keep digging*.  
- Simon infiltrates Cerberus → Finds evidence linking Gray to Ghost Protocol’s creation.  
- Wheels captured by Black Ice → Interrogation reveals the syndicate is a *proxy* for someone inside Hyperion.  

---

#### **PART 3: BREAKING GHOST PROTOCOL** *(Chapters 21-30)*  
**Confrontation & Fallout**  
- *Climax Settings:* Hyperion’s basement server farm; Black Ice’s underground data center.  

**Revelations & Action:**  
- **VP Gray Exposed:** Created Ghost Protocol to sell compromised GPUs plus ATHENA’s code to Cerberus Tech (Simon’s father) → Sabotages Hyperion for profit.  
- **Betrayal Cascade:** Elena redeems herself by cracking Gray’s encrypted ledger; Riya destroys Ghost Protocol’s core servers.  
- **Wheels’ Masterstroke:** Deploys worm to brick stolen GPUs via their firmware.  

**Thematic Resolution:**  
- **Friendship:** Team fractures but reconciles → Simon stays with Hyperion; Alex accepts leadership flaws.  
- **Technology’s Cost:** Project ATHENA shifts to civilian R&D; ethics review mandated.  
- **Conspiracy Fallout:** Gray arrested; Black Ice collapses → Media spins thefts as a "security drill" to avoid panic.  

**Final Chapter:**  
Six months later → Group founds an anti-surveillance nonprofit. Wheels quips: “Next time, steal something worthless. Like NFTs.”
